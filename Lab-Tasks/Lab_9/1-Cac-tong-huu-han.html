Các tổng hữu hạn

Given 3 following sums:

  • \(S_1(n) = 1+2^2+3^3+...+n^n. \)
  • \(S_2(n) = 1+1 \times 2 + 1 \times 2 \times 3 + ... + 1 \times 2 \times 3 \times ... \times n.\)
  • \(S_3(n) = 1 + \frac{1}{1+2} + \frac{1}{1+2+3} + ... + \frac{1}{1+2+3+...+n}.\)

Input:

  • An positive n ≥ 1

Output:

 

3 values (real numbers) corresponding to the results of the 3 sums above.
